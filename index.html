Our Proven Process

Step 1: In-depth Topic Research

ServiceDescription

Step 2: Recording the Content

ServiceDescription
Monthly Content Creation SessionsWe will conduct 1-2 monthly content creation sessions that will require about 30-120 minutes of recording time.
Interactive QuestioningWe will guide the conversation with thought-provoking questions to delve deep into each topic and extract insightful content from your knowledge base.

Step 3: Video Production

ServiceDescription
Recording Session TransformationWe convert your long-form recording session into 10-20 attention-grabbing short clips that are tailor-made for social media platforms.
Hook Selection and OptimizationIdentify and refine the most compelling moments from the recorded content to serve as hooks. We adjust clip lengths to maintain viewer attention and maximize impact.
Audio EnhancementImprove audio quality by balancing levels, reducing noise, and ensuring clarity of speech to provide a professional auditory experience.
B-Roll AdditionEnhance the visual appeal and context of clips by adding relevant B-roll footage, which complements and reinforces the message of the main content.
Caption IntegrationInclude engaging captions to each clip, ensuring they are clear and informative, enhancing accessibility and viewer retention, especially on platforms where videos are often watched without sound.

Step 4: Detailed Copywriting + Content SEO

ServiceDescription
Content Alignment for Video PostsCreate written posts that align with the concepts presented in each short video clip, ensuring a cohesive narrative across video and text formats.
Storytelling Format CreationCraft written content in a storytelling format to engage readers effectively, utilizing narrative techniques that draw the audience into the discussion.
Platform-Specific Copywriting for LinkedInDevelop thorough posts for LinkedIn, tailored to resonate well on the platform by focusing on storytelling that encapsulates a core idea, optimized to engage the professional audience.
Search-Intent Driven Titles for YouTubeConstruct video titles based on search intent, derived from the initial topic research, ensuring they are SEO-friendly and aligned with what potential viewers are searching for on YouTube.

Step 5: Content Distribution

ServiceDescription
VolumeWe use the 10-20 clips to post content on up to 6 channels of your choosing, resulting in 60-120 posts/month.
DistributionDistribute the edited content across chosen platforms (up to 6 channels), ensuring each piece is formatted and timed to meet platform-specific standards and audience preferences.
Channel OptionsUp to 6 Channels of your choosing: YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, Pinterest

ServiceDescription

  • Location-Specific Campaign Structure: Broke campaigns into tightly targeted geographies (e.g., ADU in Colorado vs. ADU in Washington), enabling granular performance control.

  • SEO-Focused Blog Strategy: Published long-tail content like "Cost to Build a Home" to attract bottom-of-funnel search traffic.
  • Conversion-Optimized Pages: Transformed location-based blogs into optimized landing pages (e.g., Home Builders Tacoma, WA) to dominate local search.
